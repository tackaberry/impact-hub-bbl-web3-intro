<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

# Blockchain

A shared (no owner), distributed database that stores data in blocks that are ultimately linked together using cryptography, making verification easy and transparent

different blockchains: Bitcoin, Ethereum

</Layout>

---

<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

## Blockchain is like the operating system<br />we need apps to make it useful

while it may seem like early days, the foundation is solid - <br />the rules, protocols, governance -<br /> everyone knows the rules and the protocols are open to anyone

</Layout>

---

<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

# Metaverse 

An immersive, iteractive virtual world when combined with blockchain technology provides a platform for application, games and virtual place-based activity
  
You can actually own the items you earn - or land you buy

</Layout>

---

<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

# Cryptocurrency 

A tradable digital asset or currency, built on blockchain technology. Uses encryption to authenticate and protect transactions. 

Examples: Ether on the Ethereum blockchain, Bitcoin, Doge

Buy and trade: Coinbase, Coinsquare, Kraken plus many more

</Layout>

---

<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

# Tokens

A token is something specific like an asset that run's on top of another cryptocurrency's blockchain. There can be many different tokens on a coin on a blockchain. Bitcoin doesn't have this capability.  Stored in Wallets. 

</Layout>

---

<Layout color={WHITE} serif light leftTitle>

# NFTs (non-fungible tokens)

A financial security consisting of digital data stored on the bloackchain. NFTs give users the ability to own objects, which can be art, photos, code, music, text, game objects, credentials, governance rights, access passes, and whatever else people dream up next.

</Layout>
